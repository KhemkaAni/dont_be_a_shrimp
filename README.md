## About

We all hunch over our screens like shrimp. This extension sends gentle reminders at your chosen interval to straighten up and fix your posture.

## Features

- **Customizable intervals** ‚Äî Choose 15, 30, 45, 60 minutes or set your own
- **Persistent settings** ‚Äî Your preference is saved across browser sessions
- **Clean notifications** ‚Äî Non-intrusive reminders that don't disrupt your flow
- **Minimal design** ‚Äî Aesthetic popup with a typewriter-style interface

## Installation

1. Download or clone this repository
2. Open Chrome and go to `chrome://extensions`
3. Enable **Developer mode** (toggle in top right)
4. Click **Load unpacked**
5. Select the `dont_be_a_shrimp` folder

## Usage

1. Click the extension icon in your browser toolbar
2. Select your preferred reminder interval
3. Or enter a custom time (1-1440 minutes)
4. Sit back and wait for your posture reminder
